## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much do Cheras nursing homes cost?

Cheras nursing homes range from RM2,200/month for basic care to RM10,000/month for premium memory care. Average is RM3,500-5,000/month.

### Why does Cheras have so many nursing homes?

Cheras has a large Chinese community with strong filial piety values, creating high demand for quality elderly care facilities.

### Are Cheras nursing homes Cantonese-speaking?

Many are - Cheras has a strong Cantonese-speaking community. Always ask about specific dialect capabilities when enquiring.

### Which Cheras area is best for nursing homes?

Taman Connaught and Taman Midah are most popular with good reputations. For budget options, consider areas near Kajang.

### Is there MRT access to Cheras nursing homes?

Yes, the MRT Sungai Buloh-Kajang line serves Cheras with stations at Taman Midah, Taman Connaught, and others.
