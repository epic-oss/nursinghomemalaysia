## Tips for Choosing a PJ Nursing Home

PJ traffic can be heavy:

Premium PJ facilities often have:

### 3. Language Options

PJ nursing homes typically offer:

- Start search 1-2 months ahead

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much do nursing homes in PJ cost?

PJ nursing homes range from RM3,500/month for basic care to RM15,000/month for premium memory care. Average is around RM5,000-7,000/month.

### Which area in PJ has the best nursing homes?

Damansara area has the most premium facilities. SS2/SS3 offers good mid-range options. Kelana Jaya is best for LRT accessibility.

### Are PJ nursing homes worth the higher cost?

If healthcare access, facility quality, and visiting convenience are priorities, PJ nursing homes offer excellent value despite higher costs.

### Is there public transport to PJ nursing homes?

Yes, facilities near Kelana Jaya and Taman Paramount have LRT access. Many areas are accessible by Grab/taxi.

### Do PJ nursing homes have English-speaking staff?

Most PJ nursing homes have English-speaking staff, more so than other areas in Klang Valley.
