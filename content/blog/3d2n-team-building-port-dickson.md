# 3 Days 2 Nights Team Building Port Dickson

Port Dickson (PD) is the closest beach destination to KL, making it perfect for 3D2N team building retreats without long travel times. With options ranging from budget chalets to the iconic Lexis Hibiscus, there's something for every team and budget.

## Why Choose Port Dickson for 3D2N Team Building?

**Key advantages:**
- Only 1.5 hours from KL
- Wide range of resort options and price points
- Beach activities without flight costs
- Good for large groups (500+ pax possible)
- Most venues are HRDF claimable

**Best for:**
- Budget-conscious beach retreats
- Large corporate groups
- Quick getaway without extensive planning
- Teams wanting beach + training combo
