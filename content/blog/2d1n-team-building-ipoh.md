# 2 Days 1 Night Team Building Ipoh

Ipoh offers a unique 2D1N experience combining heritage charm, incredible food, and easy access to Gopeng adventures. Just 2-2.5 hours from KL, it's an underrated gem for corporate retreats.

## Why Ipoh for 2D1N?

**What makes Ipoh special:**
- Beautiful heritage architecture
- Famous food scene (team bonding!)
- Cave temples and limestone scenery
- Less crowded than mainstream destinations
- Great value for money
- Close to Gopeng for adventure add-on

- Old Town Amazing Race
