# 2 Days 1 Night Team Building Port Dickson

Port Dickson (PD) is the closest beach destination to KL, making it ideal for 2D1N team building retreats. Beach activities by day, seafood dinner by night - all within 1.5 hours from the office.

## Why Port Dickson for 2D1N?

**Key advantages:**
- Only 1.5 hours from KL
- Wide range of resorts (budget to premium)
- Beach activities without flight costs
- Can accommodate large groups (500+ pax)
- Year-round suitable weather

**What you get:**
- Beach Olympics and water sports
- Sunset team bonding
- Seafood dinners
- Hotel comfort and facilities
