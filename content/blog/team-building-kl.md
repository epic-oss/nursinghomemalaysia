## Indoor Team Building Activities in KL

### Escape Rooms

Teams solve puzzles to "escape" themed rooms within time limits. Tests communication, problem-solving, and working under pressure.

**Popular venues:**
- Escape Room KL (multiple locations)
- Breakout Malaysia
- The Escape Artist

**Team size:** 4-8 per room (book multiple rooms for larger groups)

**Duration:** 60-90 minutes

**Budget:** RM60-120 per person

## Planning Team Building in KL

### Questions to Consider

1. What are your objectives? (Fun vs development)
2. What's your budget per person?
3. How much time do you have?
4. Any physical limitations in the team?
5. Indoor or outdoor preference?
6. Central location or willing to travel?

### Booking Timeline

- **4-6 weeks ahead:** Start planning for most activities
- **6-8 weeks ahead:** Peak periods (December, year-end)
- **2-3 weeks ahead:** Possible for smaller groups
- **Last minute:** Very limited options

### Red Flags

- No written quotation
- Unclear about what's included
- Can't explain the programme structure
- No references available
- Pushy sales tactics
