# 3 Days 2 Nights Team Building Janda Baik

Janda Baik is KL's best-kept secret for team building - a highland retreat just 45 minutes from the city. Escape the heat, enjoy river activities, and bond in nature without the long drive to Cameron Highlands.

## Why Choose Janda Baik for 3D2N Team Building?

**Key advantages:**
- Only 45 minutes from KL (closest highland!)
- Cool, refreshing climate
- River and nature activities
- Kampung (village) atmosphere
- Budget-friendly compared to resorts
- Minimal travel = more program time

**Best for:**
- Teams wanting nature escape without long travel
- Budget-conscious retreats
- Outdoor/adventure focus
- Small to medium groups (20-80 pax)
- Creative and startup teams

- **Waterfall visits** - Team treks to hidden falls

## HRDF Claimable Programs in Janda Baik

Janda Baik programs can be HRDF claimable with proper planning:

**Tips for HRDF compliance:**
- Engage HRDF-registered trainer/facilitator
- Document clear learning outcomes
- Ensure substantial training hours (not just activities)
- Prepare proper attendance and assessment records

**Claimable components:**
- Facilitation fees: 100%
- Accommodation: Yes (part of training package)
- Meals: Capped amount
- Transport: Yes

**Estimated HRDF claim:** 50-65% of program cost
