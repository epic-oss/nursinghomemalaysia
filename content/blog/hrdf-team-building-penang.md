## Logistics Considerations

### Getting There

**By Air:**
- Direct flights from KL (1 hour)
- Connections from most Malaysian cities
- International flights available

**By Road:**
- 4-5 hours from KL via PLUS highway
- Bridge toll applies

**By Train:**
- ETS to Butterworth (4+ hours)
- Ferry to Georgetown

### Getting Around

**Island transport:**
- Grab available island-wide
- Resort shuttles (some areas)
- Hired buses for groups
- Walking feasible in Georgetown

**Group transport recommendations:**
- 40-seater bus: RM600-900/day
- Van (10 pax): RM300-500/day
- Include airport transfers in package

### Best Timing

**Ideal months:** February-October

**Avoid:**
- November-January (monsoon, affects beach activities)
- Major holiday periods (Chinese New Year, school holidays)

**Shoulder seasons:** Good rates, fewer crowds
