## Hotel Package Pricing Guide

### What Affects Pricing

**Room type:**
- Twin sharing: Standard
- Triple sharing: 10-15% cheaper
- Single occupancy: 30-50% more

**Meal inclusions:**
- Basic: Breakfast only
- Standard: Breakfast + 1 meal
- Full board: All meals included

**Activities:**
- Self-organised: Cheapest
- Basic facilitation: Moderate
- Full professional programme: Premium

**Season:**
- Weekdays: Better rates
- Weekends: 10-20% premium
- Peak periods: 20-40% premium
- School holidays: Book early

## Negotiating Corporate Rates

### What to Ask For

**Room rates:**
- Corporate discount (typically 10-25%)
- Complimentary room upgrades
- Free rooms ratio (1 per 20-25 guests)
- Late checkout

**F&B:**
- Complimentary coffee breaks
- Upgraded menu options
- Welcome drinks
- Team dinner venue

**Facilities:**
- Free meeting room (with minimum rooms)
- Extended hours
- Additional AV equipment
- Setup/breakdown support

**Extras:**
- Airport transfers
- Activity coordination
- Photographer
- Certificates/awards

### Best Times for Deals

**Weekdays:** Monday-Thursday rates are 15-30% lower
**Off-peak:** February-March, September-October
**Last-minute:** Sometimes available 2-3 weeks out
**Early bird:** Book 8-12 weeks for best selection

### Red Flags

- Inflexible on inclusions
- Hidden charges not disclosed upfront
- Poor reviews for corporate events
- Unwilling to provide references
- No dedicated event coordinator

## Booking Process

### Timeline

**12 weeks ahead:**
- Research venue options
- Request initial proposals
- Site visit if possible

**8 weeks ahead:**
- Compare proposals
- Negotiate terms
- Confirm booking with deposit

**4 weeks ahead:**
- Finalise participant list
- Confirm dietary requirements
- Brief hotel on programme

**2 weeks ahead:**
- Final numbers confirmation
- Room allocation
- Programme confirmation

**1 week ahead:**
- Final briefing
- Share emergency contacts
- Confirm all logistics

### Questions to Ask Hotels

1. What's included in the package price?
2. What are the additional charges?
3. Is there a dedicated event coordinator?
4. What's the backup plan for outdoor activities?
5. Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?
6. What's the cancellation policy?
7. Are facilitators in-house or external?
8. What references can you provide?
