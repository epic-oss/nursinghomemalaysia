## Logistics Considerations

### Highland Access

**Cameron Highlands:**
- 3-4 hours from KL
- Winding roads
- Bus or self-drive
- Motion sickness consideration

**Genting:**
- 1 hour from KL
- Good road access
- Cable car option
- Public transport available

**Fraser's Hill:**
- 2 hours from KL
- One-way road system (check timing)
- Self-drive mainly

### East Coast Access

**Cherating:**
- 3.5-4 hours from KL
- East Coast Highway
- Bus services available

**Weather:**
- Monsoon: November-February
- Beach activities affected
- Book highlands during monsoon season

### Taman Negara Access

**Getting there:**
- Road to Kuala Tahan
- Boat option (scenic)
- 4-5 hours from KL total

**Requirements:**
- Park permits
- Licensed guides for some areas
- Advance booking essential
