## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much do nursing homes in Shah Alam cost?

Shah Alam nursing homes range from RM2,000/month for basic care to RM9,000/month for memory care. Average is RM3,000-5,000/month.

### Is Shah Alam good for nursing homes?

Yes, Shah Alam offers excellent value with quality care at lower prices than PJ or KL, plus spacious facilities and good healthcare access.

### Are there Chinese-speaking nursing homes in Shah Alam?

Yes, though fewer than in PJ or Klang. Facilities in areas bordering Klang more likely to have Chinese-speaking staff.

### How is hospital access in Shah Alam?

Good - Hospital Shah Alam provides government healthcare, and Columbia Asia offers private care. Klang General Hospital is also nearby.

### Is public transport available to Shah Alam nursing homes?

Limited - Shah Alam has minimal LRT coverage. Most families visit by car. Consider this for regular visiting logistics.
