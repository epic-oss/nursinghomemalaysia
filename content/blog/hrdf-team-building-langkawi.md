## Logistics Considerations

### Flight Booking

**Tips:**
- Book 6-8 weeks ahead for best rates
- Consider group booking discounts
- Morning flights = more programme time
- Return evening flights common

**Carriers:**
- AirAsia: Most frequent
- Malaysia Airlines: Business travel preferred
- Firefly: From Subang

### Transfers

**Airport transfers:**
- 20-40 minutes to most resorts
- Arranged by hotel or provider
- Budget RM30-80 per way for shared transfers
- Private: RM100-200 per vehicle

**Island transport:**
- Rental cars available
- Provider-arranged transport recommended
- Grab available but limited

### Weather Considerations

**Best months:** December-April
**Monsoon:** May-October (affects some activities)
**Note:** Many activities possible year-round

**Wet weather backup:**
- Indoor resort facilities
- Covered team activities
- Workshop-focused programmes
- Spa/wellness alternatives
