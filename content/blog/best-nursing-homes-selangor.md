## Selangor Nursing Home Checklist

When visiting nursing homes in Selangor, check:

**Registration & Licensing**
- JKM registration certificate displayed
- Fire department certificate
- Health ministry compliance

**Facilities**
- Air conditioning (essential in Selangor's heat)
- Backup generator
- Clean water supply
- Proper waste management

**Staff**
- Registered nurse on duty
- Caregiver-to-resident ratio (ideally 1:6 or better)
- Staff language skills (Malay/English/Chinese/Tamil)

**Safety**
- Hospital within 30 minutes
- Emergency protocols in place
- Wheelchair accessibility
- Non-slip flooring

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How many nursing homes are in Selangor?

There are over 100 registered nursing homes in Selangor, making it the state with the most options in Malaysia.

### What's the average cost of nursing homes in Selangor?

Costs range from RM2,000/month for basic care to RM10,000/month for premium facilities with specialized care.

### Which area in Selangor has the most nursing homes?

Petaling Jaya and surrounding areas (Damansara, SS2) have the highest concentration of nursing homes, followed by Klang and Shah Alam.

### Are there government nursing homes in Selangor?

Yes, there are JKM-run facilities for eligible low-income families. Contact JKM Selangor for information about subsidized care.

### How far in advance should I book a nursing home in Selangor?

For popular facilities, 1-2 months advance notice is recommended. Some premium facilities may have longer waiting lists.
