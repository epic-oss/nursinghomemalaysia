## What's Included in a Team Building Package?

Most team building packages in Malaysia include some combination of these elements:

### Core Inclusions (Usually Standard)

**Facilitator fees:** Professional trainers who run the activities, manage timing, and handle group dynamics. This is the core service you're paying for.

**Activity materials:** Props, equipment, and supplies needed for the games and challenges. Good providers invest in quality materials that don't break mid-activity.

**Programme design:** The planning and customisation work that goes into creating your specific itinerary. Better packages offer more tailoring to your objectives.

**Basic insurance:** Public liability coverage for the event. Always confirm this is included and check coverage limits.

### Common Add-Ons (Sometimes Included, Sometimes Extra)

**Venue rental:** Some packages include venue costs, others quote separately. Always clarify.

**Meals and refreshments:** Full-day packages often include lunch and coffee breaks. Half-day packages may or may not include refreshments.

**Transportation:** Rarely included unless you're booking a destination package. Usually arranged separately.

**Accommodation:** Only included in resort or retreat packages. Day programmes don't include this.

**Photography/videography:** Premium packages sometimes include event documentation. Most charge extra.

**Certificates:** Participation or completion certificates, especially for HRDF-claimable programmes.

**Post-event reports:** Debriefing documents and team assessment reports. Usually only in premium packages.

## Team Building Package Types & Pricing

### Half-Day Packages (3-4 hours)

**What you get:** 2-3 team activities, one facilitator per 20-30 participants, basic materials

**Best for:** Teams wanting team building without full-day commitment, add-on to conferences or meetings

**Typical pricing:** RM50-150 per person

**Example activities:** Icebreakers, problem-solving challenges, indoor games, simple outdoor activities

### Full-Day Packages (6-8 hours)

**What you get:** 4-6 activities, multiple facilitators, comprehensive programme, usually includes lunch

**Best for:** Most corporate team building needs, balance of depth and practicality

**Typical pricing:** RM150-300 per person

**Example activities:** Amazing Race formats, outdoor challenges, team competitions, workshops with debrief sessions

### Premium Full-Day Packages

**What you get:** Highly customised programmes, senior facilitators, premium venues, full F&B, photography

**Best for:** Executive teams, important client events, company milestones

**Typical pricing:** RM300-500 per person

**Example activities:** Luxury venue experiences, adventure activities, gourmet team cooking, yacht charters

### Overnight Retreat Packages (2D1N)

**What you get:** Full programme across two days, accommodation, all meals, evening activities

**Best for:** Teams wanting deeper bonding, strategic planning retreats, reward trips

**Typical pricing:** RM400-800 per person

**Includes:** Resort accommodation, dinner and breakfast, evening team activities, next-day programme

### Extended Retreat Packages (3D2N)

**What you get:** Comprehensive multi-day programme, resort stay, all-inclusive F&B, extensive activity schedule

**Best for:** Annual team retreats, leadership development, incentive trips

**Typical pricing:** RM700-1,500 per person

**Includes:** Two nights accommodation, all meals, full programme both days, leisure time, transportation sometimes included

## How to Compare Team Building Packages

### Ask These Questions

**What exactly is included?**
Get a detailed breakdown, not just "full-day team building package." Ask specifically about venue, meals, materials, and insurance.

**How many facilitators for my group size?**
Good ratio is 1 facilitator per 20-30 participants for simple activities, 1 per 10-15 for complex programmes. Understaffed events feel chaotic.

**What's the facilitator's background?**
Experienced corporate trainers versus part-time event staff make a significant difference. Ask about qualifications and years of experience.

**Is the programme customised or off-the-shelf?**
Better packages include consultation to understand your objectives and tailor activities accordingly. Generic programmes may not address your specific team needs.

**What happens if it rains?**
For outdoor packages, understand the backup plan. Good providers have indoor alternatives ready.

**Are there hidden costs?**
Ask about setup fees, overtime charges, additional facilitator fees, and whether quoted prices include SST.

### Red Flags to Watch

- Prices significantly below market rate
- Reluctance to provide detailed breakdown
- No insurance or unclear coverage
- Single facilitator for large groups
- No customisation offered
- Poor reviews or no references available

### Green Flags

- Clear, itemised quotations
- Willing to provide references
- Consultation call before quoting
- Flexible to your objectives
- Professional communication
- Comprehensive insurance coverage

## HRDF-Claimable Packages

Malaysian companies registered with HRDF can claim 50-80% of qualifying team building costs. HRDF-claimable packages typically include:

- Registered trainer conducting the programme
- Structured learning outcomes and assessments
- Documentation for claims submission
- Certificates of completion

**Important:** Not all team building packages qualify. Specifically ask vendors about HRDF eligibility and what documentation they provide.

HRDF packages often cost 10-20% more than non-claimable equivalents due to additional compliance requirements, but the claimback more than compensates.

## Budgeting Tips

### For Small Budgets (Under RM100/person)

- Choose half-day programmes
- Consider indoor venues (no rental costs)
- Book during off-peak periods (avoid Dec, CNY)
- Larger groups get better per-person rates

### For Medium Budgets (RM100-300/person)

- Full-day programmes become accessible
- Outdoor and adventure options available
- Lunch inclusion is standard
- Some customisation possible

### For Larger Budgets (RM300+/person)

- Premium venues and experiences
- Overnight retreats possible
- Full customisation expected
- Photography and documentation included
- HRDF-claimable options widely available
