# 2 Days 1 Night Team Building Janda Baik

Janda Baik is KL's best-kept secret for quick overnight retreats. Just 45 minutes from the city, enjoy highland air, river activities, and kampung charm - perfect for a 2D1N team escape.

## Why Janda Baik for 2D1N?

**Perfect for overnight trips because:**
- Only 45 minutes from KL (closest highland!)
- Arrive early, maximize program time
- Cooler evening temperatures
- No need for long drives
- Great value for money

**What makes it special:**
- River and waterfall activities
- Kampung (village) atmosphere
- Escape city without long travel
- Nature immersion close to home
