# Tadom Hill Team Building: Complete Guide & Packages {{currentYear}}

## What is Tadom Hill Resort?

Located in Banting, Selangor (just 45 minutes from Kuala Lumpur), Tadom Hill Resort is an eco-adventure park built around former mining lakes, creating a stunning turquoise water landscape perfect for outdoor team activities. The resort combines natural beauty with adrenaline-pumping activities, making it ideal for corporate team building events.

**Quick Facts:**
- **Location:** Banting, Selangor (accessible from KL, Shah Alam, Petaling Jaya)
- **Size:** Multiple activity zones across 200+ acres
- **Capacity:** Can accommodate teams from 20 to 200+ participants
- **Operating Hours:** 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM daily
- **Best for:** Adventure-seeking teams, outdoor enthusiasts, mixed skill levels

## Popular Team Building Activities at Tadom Hill

### Must-Try Activities

**Kayaking Adventure (60-90 minutes)**
Team members navigate the turquoise lakes in pairs or small groups. Excellent for:
- Communication skills
- Coordination and synchronization
- Trust building between kayak partners
- Problem-solving (navigating courses)

**Difficulty:** Easy to Moderate
**Team size:** 2-4 per kayak
**Physical requirement:** Basic swimming ability recommended

**High Ropes Challenge (60-90 minutes)**
Navigate elevated obstacle courses 10-15 meters above ground:
- Tests courage and conquers fears
- Builds trust (team members belay each other)
- Develops mutual support culture
- Leadership opportunities (encouraging teammates)

**Difficulty:** Moderate to Challenging
**Team size:** Individual challenge with team support
**Physical requirement:** Moderate fitness, no fear of heights

**Team Building Games & Challenges (90-120 minutes)**
Customized ground activities designed by facilitators:
- **The Great Race:** Teams compete through activity stations
- **Raft Building:** Construct and test rafts using limited materials
- **Blind Navigation:** Trust exercises using blindfolds and verbal guidance
- **Human Knot:** Classic team problem-solving activity

**Difficulty:** Adjustable to team capability
**Team size:** 8-15 per group
**Physical requirement:** Minimal

**ATV Adventure Trail (45-60 minutes)**
Navigate ATVs through jungle paths and challenging terrain:
- Builds confidence
- Rewards calculated risk-taking
- Creates exciting shared memories
- Ice-breaker for new teams

**Difficulty:** Easy (full training provided)
**Team size:** 1-2 per ATV
**Physical requirement:** Basic coordination

**Stand-Up Paddleboarding (SUP) (45-60 minutes)**
Balance on boards while paddling across calm lake sections:
- Focus and concentration
- Individual challenge with team encouragement
- Great for photos and social media moments
- Low-pressure fun activity

**Difficulty:** Easy to Moderate
**Team size:** Individual
**Physical requirement:** Good balance helps

## Safety Measures & Considerations

Tadom Hill prioritizes participant safety:

**Safety Equipment:**
- Life jackets for all water activities (compulsory)
- Helmets for high ropes, climbing, ATVs
- Safety harnesses inspected daily
- First aid kits at every activity station

**Professional Staff:**
- Certified activity instructors
- Safety briefings before each activity
- Lifeguards on duty at water areas
- First aid trained facilitators

**Weather Protocols:**
- Activities paused during lightning/thunderstorms
- Covered areas available for team games during light rain
- Flexible scheduling to accommodate weather changes

**Insurance:**
- Group insurance typically included in packages
- Verify coverage details when booking
- Personal accident insurance recommended for comprehensive coverage

**Medical Considerations:**
- Inform organizers of participants with medical conditions
- Heart conditions, pregnancy, recent surgeries may restrict certain activities
- Epilepsy, severe vertigo should skip high-rope activities
- Swimming ability required for deep-water kayaking zones

### When to Book

**Peak Season (Higher Prices, Book Early):**
- June - August (school holidays)
- October - November (post-monsoon, perfect weather)
- December (year-end company events)

**Off-Peak Advantages:**
- January - February (better rates, less crowded)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can non-swimmers participate in water activities?
Yes! Life jackets are compulsory for all water activities. Kayaking and SUP are in relatively calm lake waters. However, inform facilitators about non-swimmers so they receive extra attention.

### What happens if it rains on our booking date?
Light rain: Most activities continue with waterproof gear provided.
Heavy rain/storms: Activities pause; team can move to covered areas for indoor team games.
Severe weather: Rescheduling options available depending on your booking terms.

### Is Tadom Hill suitable for older employees or those with limited fitness?
Yes! Activity difficulty can be adjusted. Select easier options like gentle kayaking, ground-based team games, and skip high ropes/climbing. Most packages allow activity substitutions.

### Can we bring our own food and drinks?
Generally no. Tadom Hill works with caterers for hygiene and waste management. However, dietary requirements (vegetarian, halal, allergies) can be accommodated with advance notice.

### How far in advance should we book for a group of 80 people?
Recommended: 6-8 weeks for weekend bookings, 4-6 weeks for weekdays. This ensures activity slot availability and gives time for HRDF applications if needed.

### Is photography allowed?
Yes! Photo opportunities abound. Waterproof phone cases recommended for water activities. Many packages include professional group photography.

### What age range is suitable for Tadom Hill activities?
Most activities suitable for ages 12 and above. Some easier activities (kayaking, team games) work for ages 10+. High-rope courses typically require participants to be at least 1.3m tall.

### Are there toilets and changing facilities?
Yes. Clean toilet facilities and changing rooms with lockers available. Shower facilities also provided (bring your own toiletries).
