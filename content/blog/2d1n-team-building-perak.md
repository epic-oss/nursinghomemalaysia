# 2 Days 1 Night Team Building Perak

Perak offers diverse 2D1N options - from white water rafting in Gopeng to heritage charm in Ipoh. Perfect for teams wanting adventure or culture (or both!) within a weekend getaway.

## Why Perak for 2D1N?

**What Perak offers:**
- Best white water rafting in Peninsular Malaysia
- Unique limestone caves
- Heritage city experiences (Ipoh)
- Less crowded, better value
- Easy 2-2.5 hour drive from KL

## 2D1N Destinations in Perak

### 1. Gopeng (Adventure)

**Best for:** Adrenaline seekers, sales teams, memorable experiences

**Distance from KL:** 2 hours

**Signature activities:**
- White water rafting (Grade 2-3)
- Cave exploration
- Waterfall abseiling
- River tubing

**Budget:** RM350-550/pax

### 2. Ipoh (Heritage & Food)

**Best for:** Culture lovers, food teams, comfortable retreats

**Distance from KL:** 2-2.5 hours

**Signature activities:**
- Heritage Amazing Race
- Food trails
- Cave temples
- Street art exploration

**Budget:** RM250-450/pax

## Gopeng Rafting - What to Know

**The experience:**
- Grade 2-3 rapids (exciting but safe)
- 3-4 hour journey
- Professional guides
- All equipment provided

**Requirements:**
- Basic swimming ability
- Minimum age usually 7-10 years
- No serious health conditions

**What's included:**
- Safety equipment (helmet, life jacket)
- Professional guide
- Transport to/from rafting point
- Usually includes lunch

**Why teams love it:**
- Unforgettable shared experience
- Tests teamwork under pressure
- Great stories to tell back at office
- Photos and videos to remember

## HRDF Claimable Programs

Perak programs can be HRDF claimable:

**Gopeng:** Many adventure providers are HRDF registered. Rafting can be framed as "team dynamics training."

**Ipoh:** Good for training-focused programs with comfortable hotel facilities.

**Estimated claim:** 45-65% depending on structure
