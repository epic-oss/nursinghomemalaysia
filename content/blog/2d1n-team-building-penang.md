# 2 Days 1 Night Team Building Penang

Penang offers a unique 2D1N team building experience combining heritage exploration, beach activities, and incredible food. Perfect for northern Malaysia companies or KL teams wanting a short flight getaway.

## Why Penang for 2D1N?

**What makes Penang special:**
- UNESCO World Heritage George Town
- Beautiful beaches (Batu Ferringhi)
- Famous food scene (team bonding over meals!)
- Mix of culture and relaxation
- Good for both local and flying teams

**Access options:**
- Drive from northern Malaysia (2-4 hours)
- 1-hour flight from KL
- Ferry from Butterworth

## Best 2D1N Locations in Penang

### Beach Resorts (Batu Ferringhi)

**Shangri-La Rasa Sayang**
- Premium beachfront resort

- Budget: RM500-700/pax

- Budget: RM350-450/pax

- Solid mid-range option

## 2D1N Activities in Penang

### Heritage Activities
- George Town Amazing Race
- Street art treasure hunt
- Heritage walking tour
- Clan jetty exploration
- Temple & mosque visits

### Beach Activities
- Beach Olympics
- Water sports at Batu Ferringhi
- Banana boat, parasailing
- Beach volleyball

### Food Experiences
- Hawker food trail challenge
- Cooking class competition
- Nasi kandar team dinner
- Nyonya cuisine experience

### Other Activities
- Penang Hill team hike
- Kek Lok Si Temple visit
- Tropical Spice Garden
- Escape Theme Park (team challenges)

## Getting to Penang

### From KL
- **Flight:** 1 hour, RM100-250 return (book early)
- **Drive:** 4 hours via PLUS highway
- **Bus:** 5 hours, budget option

### From Northern Malaysia
- Kedah: 1-2 hours drive
- Perak: 2-3 hours drive
- Perlis: 2 hours drive
